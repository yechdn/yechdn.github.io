Philosophy & Vision

I ultimately want to improve the world through technology entrepreneurship. We live barely a century; if we compress our life’s timeline from years to decades or centuries, we appear for only a moment and disappear again. I therefore seek something more enduring.

By founding technology ventures I hope that my intent will outlive me—however slightly—and I am grateful for the chance to leave a meaningful trace even through repeated failures.

Tomorrow’s technologies will likely be even more centralized and power-concentrated than today. My company’s very purpose is to narrow that gap, and I plan to transfer its ownership to a public-interest foundation so it can keep working toward a fairer and more beautiful world long after me.

Finally, I hope the resolve I hold now, while still young, will remain unshaken as the years pass.
