Yechan Choi

Hi, my name is Yechan Choi and I am a junior at Seoul National University. I am currently an undergraduate intern at the Multiscale Biomedical Engineering Laboratory (MBEL) under the supervision of Professor Noo-Li Jeon.

Philosophy & Vision

I ultimately want to make the world a better place through technology entrepreneurship. Life is fleeting when viewed on the scale of decades or centuries; I seek something more enduring. Through tech ventures, I hope my will can persist beyond my own life, even if only slightly, and I am grateful for the chance to leave a meaningful trail—even after failures.

As future technologies become ever more centralized and power-concentrated, my goal is to build a company whose mission is to narrow that very divide. In the long term I plan to transfer its ownership to a public-interest foundation, so it can keep pursuing a more equitable and beautiful world.

Lastly, I hope the conviction I hold now—while still young—will remain with me as I grow older.
